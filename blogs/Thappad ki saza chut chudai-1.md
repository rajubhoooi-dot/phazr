TITLE: Thappad ki saza chut chudai-1

Main ek gareeb family se hu. Badi mehnat se pehle maine padhayi ki, aur fir exam clear karke sarkari job par lagi. Mere ghar waale bahut khush the. Ye sarkari job hamare tang halaat ko theek karne wali thi.
Meri posting mere gaon ke school mein hui. Wahan bachhe zyada padhne mein interested nahi hote, isliye sakhti baratni padti hai, aur kabhi-kabhi hath bhi uthana padta hai. Maine apni class ke saare bachho ko ache se seedha kar diya, aur unko padhne ki aadat daal di. Unke parents bhi hamesha mera shukriya karte the.
Ek din hamare Headmaster ne mujhe aur ek aur teacher ko office mein bulaya. Wo hame kisi training par bhejna chaahte the, jo education department ne organise ki thi. Hamare school ke best teachers ko bheja jana tha, aur unme ek main thi. Iske liye hame 2 din ke liye shehar ke ek college mein teaching practice karni thi.
Maine ghar pe bata diya, aur agle din mein doosri teacher ke sath shehar chali gayi. Wahan college walo ne hamare stay ka acha intezaam kar rakha tha. Thodi der aaram karne ke baad hum log training ke liye chale gaye. 2 ghante ki training ke baad hame teaching practice ke liye alag-alag classes mein bheja gaya.
Mujhe jis class mein bheja gaya, wahan second year ke students baithe the. Zyadatar boys the, aur 3-4 girls thi. Maine wahan jaa kar full confidence mein padhana shuru kar diya. Kaafi bachhe meri baat sunne lage, lekin ek ladka bahut shararatein kar raha tha.
Maine usko 2-3 baar mana kiya, lekin usne meri baat ko ignore kiya. Wo sath wali line mein baithi ladki ko pareshan kar raha tha kagaz fenk-fenk ke. Mujhe us par gussa aa gaya. Fir jaise main apni class ke bachho ko thappad laga deti thi, maine usko bhi laga diya. Wo ladka hairan ho gaya, aur sab us par hasne lage.
Fir wo utha, aur class ke baahar chala gaya. Maine fir se padhana shuru kar diya. Kuch der mein college ka peon mere paas aaya, aur bola ki mujhe principal ne bulaya tha. Main principal office mein gayi. Wahan wo mujhe bola-
Principal: Madam ye aapne kya kiya. Sanjay Avasthi ko thappad maar diya.
Main: Sir wo ladka class mein disturbance kar raha tha.
Principal: Aap jaanti hai Sanjay kon hai?
Main: Kon hai?
Principal: Abhi ke MP ka beta hai. Wo aadmi bahut powerful hai. Agar usko pata chala ki aapne aisa kiya hai uske bete ke sath to aapki job bhi jaa sakti hai..
Main: Aise kaise jaa sakti hai? Mera paas government ka appointment letter hai.
Principal: Usse koi farak nahi padta. Wo bahut powerful hai, kuch bhi kar sakta hai.
Principal ki baatein sun kar meri gaand fatne lagi. Maine socha ki agar sach mein meri job chali gayi, to mere parivar ka kya hoga. Fir maine principal se poocha-
Main: Lekin sir galti to us ladke ki thi. Aur maine jaan-boojh kar usko nahi maara. Mujhe pata hota ki wo MP ka beta hai, to main usko nahi maarti. Ab aap hi batao iska solution kya hoga.
Abhi Principal bolne hi wala tha, ki tabhi uska phone baj utha. Phone utha kar principal bola-
Principal: Hello. Ji namaskaar sir. Sir wo madam hai, gaon se training ke liye aayi hai. Nahi sir, unko is baat ka pata nahi tha ki Sanjay aapka beta hai. Baat karenge sir. Abhi karwata hu.
Fir Principal ne phone ka receiver mere aage kiya, aur bola: Minister sahab baat karna chaahte hai aapse.
Meri gaand fatt rahi thi. Maine kaampte hue hatho se phone ka receiver hath mein liya. Fir boli-
Main: Hello Sir.
Minister: Madam ya kya kiya aapne? Aapko pata hai na govt. Rules ke hisab se bachho par hath nahi uthana chahiye. Fir bhi aapne ye kiya. Aapko apni naukri pyari hai ki nahi?
Main: Sir bas thodi si galti ho gayi. Sir main aapse maafi maangti hu. Aage se aisa nahi hoga.
Minister: Dekhiye aapne thappad Sanjay ko maara hai, to sorry bhi usko boliye. Agar usne maaf kiya to theek. Nahi to aapki naukari to hum rehne nahi denge.
Main: Nahi sir, main badi mushkil se yahan tak pahunchi hu. Main Sanjay se maafi maang loongi. Aap batao kab aur kahan maafi maangni hai.
Minister: Main Sanjay ko bolta hu. Dekhte hai wo kya kehta hai.
Ye bol kar minister ne phone cut kar diya. Ab main principal ki taraf dekhne lagi. Principal bola-
Principal: Ye to achi baat hai wo aapko maafi maangne ka ek mauka de raha hai. Lekin Sanjay bahut harami ladka hai. Pata nahi wo kya karega.
Ab mere andar ghabrahat badhti jaa rahi thi. Main soch rahi thi ki wo jo kuch bhi kahega main karungi. Lekin kisi bhi haal mein main apni job nahi jaane doongi.
Fir main apne room mein chali gayi. Kuch der baar mujhe ek unknown number se call aayi. Maine phone uthaya to saamne Sanjay tha phone par. Jaise hi usne mujhe kaha ki wo Sanjay bol raha tha, to maine usko bola-
Main: Sanjay beta mujhe maaf kar dena. Mujhse galti ho gayi. Mujhe pata nahi tha tum kon ho.
Sanjay: Madam ye baat to sahi nahi hai. Aap ne meri insult sab ke saamne kari thi. To maafi bhi sab ke saamne hi maangni padegi.
Main: Haa tum bata do, kahan maangni hai. Main maang loongi.
Sanjay: Subha ki morning assembly mein aapko sab ke saamne mujhse maafi maangni padegi.
Main: Koi dikkat nahi hai. Ab galti hui hai to maafi maangni banti hai. Main maang loongi.
Sanjay: Theek hai, to kal milte hai.
Doston aaj ki kahani yahin tak. Aage ki kahani agle part mein.
