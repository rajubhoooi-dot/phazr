Kyunki ladkiyon ki tarakki ke raaste mein lund hi hote hai, jinko doosri bhasha mein rukawatein bol dete hai. Jab maine job shuru ki thi, to meri chut kuwari thi, aur main bholi bhaali thi. Lekin manager bante-bante main chaalu ban gayi, aur meri kuwari chut bhonsda ban gayi. Wahin safar main aapke sath share karne wali hu. To chaliye shuru karte hai.
Meri job aaj se 4 saal pehle as a Junior Sales Executive lagi thi. Mera senior ek 32 saal ka ladka tha, mujhe jisko assist karna tha sales ke liye. Us waqt mera figure 34-29-34 tha. Rang to mera gora hai hi, aur nain-naksh bhi khoobsurat hai.
Mere senior ka naam Vikram tha. Wo kaafi smart tha, aur handsome tha. Usne gym jaa kar achi physique bhi banayi hui thi. Aur wo abhi tak single tha. Lekin ye usne sab ko bata rakha tha ki wo single tha. Jab ki sach ye tha ki uski engagement ho rakhi thi. Mujhe ye tab pata chala, jab pyar ke naam par usne meri chut faad di.
Nayi-nayi office mein join karke main bahut khush thi. Main tight pants aur shirt pehan kar office jati thi. Saara staff acha tha, aur Vikram Sir to bahut hi ache the. Wo bade pyar se sab samjhate the, aur har waqt mazaak karte rehte the. Unke sath time ka pata hi nahi chalta tha. Aisa lagta hi nahi tha ki naukri kar rahe the, aisa lagta tha jaise masti karne office jaate the.
Main us waqt bholi, kapti duniya se anjaan, aur kuwari kali thi. Ab is umar mein aisa achi personality wala ladka mile to dil aa hi jayega us par. Bhale hi wo umar mein bada tha, lekin pyar mein padi ladki ye sab kahan sochti hai.
Aise hi 3 maheene beet gaye, aur main Vikram sir ke sath apni jodi banne ke sapne dekhti rehti. Jin pyar bhari nazron se main usko dekhti thi, shayad usko bhi pata chal gaya tha ki mere mann mein uske liye pyar tha. Fir ek din sir mere paas aaye aur bole-
Vikram: Kamini ek good news hai.
Main: Kya sir?
Vikram: Hamari ek deal Shimla mein hone wali hai. Boss ne mujhe wo deal final karne ke liye bola hai. Sath mein mujhe ek assistant leke jana hai. Agar tum kaho to tumhara naam dedu? Agar deal ho gayi, to tumhara promotion pakka.
Main: Main chalungi sir, lekin promotion ke liye nahi. Aapko assist karne ke liye.
Aur ye bol kar maine pyar bhari nazron se sir ki taraf dekha. Unko shayad kuch samajh nahi aaya, to unhone kaha-
Vikram: Matlab jaogi na?
Main: Ji sir.
Vikram: Chalo theek hai, fir main tumhara naam recommend kar deta hu.
Hame 2 din baad nikalna tha. Maine ghar par bol diya, aur packing kar li. Jis din jana tha, us din subha hi hum nikal pade. Hum sir ki gaadi se jaane waale the. Sir ne mujhe mere ghar se pick kiya, aur hum nikal gaye Shimla ke safar par. Sir drive kar rahe the, aur main unke sath wali seat par baithi thi. Main poori unki wife wali feeling lete hue baar-baar unko paani cold drink pooch rahi thi.
8 ghante ka safar tha. 2 ghante baad hum ek restaurant par nashta karne ke liye ruke. Wahan se thodi der mein nashta karke nikal pade. Fir sir bole-
Vikram: Aur batao Kamini apne baare mein.
Main: Aap poochiye sir.
Vikram: Hmm, main kya poochu? Tum khud hi bata do kuch.
Main: Sir ab mujhe kaise pata hoga ki aap kya jaanna chaahte ho.
Vikram: Chalo boyfriend ke baare mein batao. Tumhara koi boyfriend hai kya?
Main: Nahi sir.
Vikram: Kyun nahi? Itni achi ladki ho tum. Tumhara boyfriend na ho aisa kaise?
Main: Bas aise hi sir. Mujhe koi aisa mila nahi, jo mere dil ko choo jaye.
Vikram: Oh, tum to badi gehri baat bol rahi ho. To fir kaisa ladka hoga jo dil ko choo jayega?
Main: Sir jo handsome ho, smart ho, hardwork karne wala ho. Meri care kare, aur mujhe bahut hasaye.
Vikram: Acha to jis ladke mein ye saari qualities hongi, tum uski girlfriend banogi?
Main: Yes sir.
Vikram: Ye qualities to mujhme bhi hai, to kya tum meri girlfriend ban jaogi?
Ye bol kar sir hasne lage. Fir main boli-
Main: Sir main to kab se banne ko taiyaar hu, lekin aap ho ki samajhte hi nahi.
Ye sunte hi sir ne meri taraf hairani se dekha. Fir wo hasne lage aur bole-
Vikram: Tum bhi punch maar rahi ho!
Main: Nahi sir, main serious hu.
Vikram sir fir se mujhe hairani se dekhne lage. Wo bole-
Vikram: Tum mujhe pasand karti ho?
Main: Hmm.
Vikram: Mujhe apna boyfriend banana chaahti ho?
Main: Main zindagi bhar ke liye aapka sath chaahti hu. I love you Vikram.
Mere muh se I love you sunte hi Vikram sir ne gaadi rok di. Hum log highway ki side mein ruke the, aur hame dekhne wala aas-paas koi nahi tha. Fir Vikram sir bole-
Vikram: Tum sach keh rahi ho Kamini?
Main: Ji, bilkul sach.
Aur ye kehte hue maine apna hath unke hath mein rakh diya. Fir Vikram sir mere kareeb aaye, aur meri aankhon mein aankhen daal kar bole-
Vikram: I love you too Kamini. I am so lucky, jo itni khoobsurat ladki ne mujhe apna boyfriend banaya.
Ye keh kar wo mere aur kareeb aane lage. Main samajh gayi thi, ki wo mujhe kiss karne waale the. Maine kabhi kiss nahi kiya tha, bas films mein dekha tha. To jaise heroine hero ke paas aane par apni aankhen band kare leti hai, waise hi maine bhi apni aankhen band kar li.
Fir Vikram sir ne apne honth mere hontho se chipka diye.
